These files are copied from the excel workbooks that are the most recent ones available as of July 2026.
They end in "Rev2022G" or "Rev2022F" depending on the workbook. These files are copies of the independent
watershed just before the s-curve step (called 'input_to_s_curve') and the dependent watershed just after
(called 'output_from_s_curve'). These sheets exist for verification purposes to check that the logic of
the Python scripts exactly matches the Excel leading up to the s-curve, and then from after the s-curve
to the end of the process. 

See other documentation for why the Python s-curve does not always exactly replicate the sheets.
